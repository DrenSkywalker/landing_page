How it works

Have a nice, quick and safe journey with few steps.

  • Open your app

    Open your app, choose your destination, verify your ride settings such as size of the dragon, price and estimated arrival time. Choose whatever ride you like and confirm!

  • Wait for your ride

    The nearest tider will accept your reservation and you'll receive a notification on your app one minute before the dragon arrives.

  • Your ride begins

    After the dragon arrives, you and the tider must verify your reservation. If everything is okay, the ride begins!

  • Safe destination

    During the journey the tider can access the app to follow the right directions.

  • Time for a feedback

    At the end of your journey you and the tider can leave a negative or positive feedback at each other through the app.

Safety

We care about every customer and tiders.

  • Protection from any danger

    Every dragon is equipped with a spells free magic seal. You don't need to worry about casually casting spells or about the sharp blade of your sword. We serve every customer, be they necromancers or healers, sorcerers or warriors!

  • User your app for safety and sharing

    With our app you can request assistance and call for emergency with a simple touch. You can share your ride details with your loved ones.

  • A community made by you

    Milions of users and tiders are commited to follow the community guidelines and take responsibility in respecting and enforcing them. We provide an online community managed by people like you, with guides and support.

  • Receive assistance at any time

    We have a team of experts available 32 hours a day, 8 moon cycle a week. Contact them with your app at any time for questions or concerns about your safety.

  • CPS monitoring

    All tiders are tracked via our Clairvoyance Positioning System from start to finish so that no matter what happens, there is a trace.
